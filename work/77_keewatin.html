77 Keewatin

This project took the surrounding community, and contextualized it as simple, highly functional apartments.

Client

Verdiroc Development Corporation

Location

Toronto, Ontario

Size

8 Stories - 80 Suites

Project

Apartments & Building Amenities

Role

Project Architect [Turner-Fleischer]

Keewatin Avenue is mostly a single family neighbourhood with homes of varying sizes and age. This 8 storey rental apartment building is located close to Yonge street and is designed to link with the existing 40 storey apartment building at 88 Erskine Avenue to the south also owned by Verdiroc. The building has a range of suite sizes from small studios to three bedroom. There is a gym and amenity area with a small outdoor patio in the basement level. The link takes place at the existing underground parking which will be shared with the two buildings. To match the neighbourhood vernacular, the building is clad with brick and stone.

The Keewatin Avenue frontage is a four storey building with two storey townhouse apartment units at ground level lowering the building massing down to the single family home scale,. The eight storey building is set back from Keewatin Avenue.
