Villa Bahati

Located on the sunny Caribbean island of Aruba, this custom estate was designed and built entirely by our team.

Client

Michele van der Wal

Location

Borancana, Aruba

Size

3 400 sf

Project

5 Bedrooms, 6 Bathrooms & Marketing

Role

Architect, Interiors & Landscaping

Bahati means fortune or luck in Swahili, the national language in Kenya. This may not relate to Aruba but the owners of the property were brought up there and so adopted their favorite areas of that country for the bedroom names.

Located inland and five minute drive to the beach, the villa has 5 bedrooms each with its own ensuite bathroom, a large kitchen, a spacious living room and a covered terrace overlooking a landscaped back yard and swimming pool. The central living areas with exposed trusses and high ceilings are tucked between two rectangular wings which accommodate the bedrooms. The layout is stepped to follow the sloped land and the rectangular design with intersecting corridors provide vistas to exterior through large windows.

All the interior wood furniture was custom designed and built in Bali, Indonesia along with stone sinks, vanities and kitchen counters.
