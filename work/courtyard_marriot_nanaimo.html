Courtyard Marriot in Nanaimo

Extending a hotel chains presence in BC.

This project is currently under construction.

Client

PEG Companies

Location

Nanaimo, British Columbia

Size

116 000 sf

Project

Hotel & Building Amenities

Role

Project Architect [Turner-Fleischer]

Located directly across from the Nanaimo Civic Centre, and just a few steps from the ocean, this small site is a perfect location for a hotel squeezed between three streets and a cliff. The shape and size of the site have dictated the overall building form with minimal at grade setbacks to the streets. The hotel tower aligns with the park on the east property line, and the street line to the south. Marriott has chosen their Courtyard brand for this site, which has various options for colors and materials both interior and exterior.

The 9 storey hotel features 155 rooms on 8 floors above ground floor public spaces including a bistro restaurant, meeting room, swimming pool and gym. Large overhead glass doors will open up the interior allowing the bistro space to flow out onto a landscaped patio. The street façade includes a wood trellis structure and stone cladding, which embrace the traditional style of the British Columbia culture.
