Toronto Rock Athletic Centre

Creating a place for training and betterment.

Client

Bachly Construction

Location

Oakville, Ontario

Size

82 000 sf

Project

Institutional - Athletic Centre

Role

Project Architect [Turner-Fleischer]

The training centre for this professional lacrosse team is located in Oakville, Ontario with two arenas, offices, a gym and associated sports facilities. The two arenas are located in one large open span steel framed structure divided by change rooms, gym and lounge. Administration change rooms and a small retail component are located in a two storey structure as a frontage to the facility. The entrance portal with the team’s colours portray the power and strength associated with this sport.
