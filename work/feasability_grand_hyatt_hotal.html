Grand Hyatt Hotal [Feasibility Study]

Schematic and conceptual design work to see if Hyatt could establish it's own hotel vision in Aruba.

Client

Royal Group of Companies

Location

Frigate Bay, Saint Kitts

Size

20 Acres

Project

Planning & Urban Design

Role

Project Architect [Turner-Fleischer]

The site is located on the north area of the island fronting west onto the Caribbean Sea and close to Harashi Beach, a popular area for local residents. Just east of the site and higher up is the Tierra Del Sol residential development and golf course and being a low rise resort would not block views from homes to the sea.

Hyatt Hotels have two locations in Aruba and both are high rise. The vision for the Grand Hyatt was to have an island feel with low rise central core and guest rooms spread out in 2 and 3 floor blocks. The program was to have 50 time-share units and 250 guest rooms. The central core included restaurants, banquet hall, bars, SPA/gym and a large salt-water swimming pool. The time share blocks are located in the south wing and the guest rooms in the north. Both wings have smaller swimming pools centred in clusters of blocks. As part of the resort, a channel was to be cut from the sea as a waterway for boats to access a marina and yacht club.
