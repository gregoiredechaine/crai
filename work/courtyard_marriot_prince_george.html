Courtyard Marriot in Prince George

Designing outside the lines for an established hotel.

Client

PEG Companies

Location

Prince George, British Columbia

Size

116 000 sf

Project

Hotel & Building Amenities

Role

Project Architect [Turner-Fleischer]

Situated in the heart of Prince George, Marriott’s largest brand, Courtyard, is designed for the business traveller and as such is well positioned as neighbour to the City’s Civic Centre. While adopting only the Courtyard exterior colour pallet, the design of this 6 storey wood framed building did not use the Courtyard prototypical layout. The program and site dictated a layout and structure not compatible to the norm. The 174 rooms are located on 5 wood framed floors on a concrete and steel framed 35,000 sf ground floor podium. The podium includes the main lobby and administration, 6,100 sf conference/ballroom area, bistro, swimming pool, gym and laundry. The street façade includes a wood trellis structure and stone cladding, which embrace the traditional architectural style and culture of British Columbia.

The hotel also includes an underground parking for 91 cars with direct elevator access to the main lobby.
